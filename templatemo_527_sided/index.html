My Idea

One idea that I have thought about is a food ordering/deliver app that is more visual-focused. The app would follow a similar user interface as Tinder, where people see pictures of food, and they can swipe left or right depending on whether or not they want to order the food. I got the idea from looking at foodstas on Instagram. Whenever I wanted to order food, I would have to go to the restaurant's Instagram, and from there, I would either have to go to a third party app or the restaurant's website. This app would facilitate that process, so that people can order directly after seeing some food that looks appetizing. We tend to eat with our eyes first, so I think this food ordering/delivery app will help combine the visual aspect of eating with the more practical aspect of ordering food.
